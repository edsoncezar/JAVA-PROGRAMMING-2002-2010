Using Email at SBU

Communication with your tutors is often best done by email, and they will expect you to regularly check for messages from them

  • It is the policy of the University that communication will be done through your SBU account, and you cannot assume we will use your existing account (e.g. Hotmail, AOL, BT or Virgin)
  • To send email using your SBU account, you have to be logged in using your SBU supplied account. Pegasus mail is available on the laboratory machines, ( there is a 1 Mb limit on the size of attatchments). Click on this link for instructions Using Pegasus
  • It is possible to redirect your email from the VAX system to other systems, and you can find details of this by clicking on this link :Transferring your email account
