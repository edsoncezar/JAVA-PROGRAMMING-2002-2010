Schedule for Waypoint 1

This series of activities will allow you to get started using the NT workstations to develop Java programs and progress to Waypoint 2

You should follow the activites in the sequence listed below, recording your progress in your workbook and keeping notes in your notebook

Waypoint 1 begins with a number of small case studies of designing, implementing, demonstrating and extending some simple Java Classes. the SSD lectures will reinforce some of this material and will also supply the context for how and why classes are used

This is introductory material and you are  expected to be able to finish it in no more than 4 weeks
