Simple Advice

The advice below is simple and could save much pain in the future

  1. Leaving your work on a machine in the labs is an invitation for someone to copy, so delete your work before you shut down the machine. There are heavy penalties for copying at this University don't be an innocent victim
  2. If you want to keep your files secret, use an encryption program
  3. Editing, compiling or running a program from a floppy disk is asking for trouble. It will fail eventually!
  4. Create and keep your work in a safe work area on the machine, in your own folder.
  5. Keeps lots of copies of your work,
    • in different places and on different media.
    • assume you will loose work (probably at the worst time!).
    • Loosing work is not an excuse for missing work or not getting assessments in on time. (You will be asked to submit your backup copy if you loose your work)
  6. Use email with your sbu account to contact your tutors.
  7. Check your email regularly. Your tutors will contact you through this email account
  8. Read the section on how to choose a good password link to the password page
  9. On days when assessments are due for submission, there is usually a big queue just before the deadline time. The deadline time still applies. You cannot blame the queue. You could even hand it in early!
  10. If you have a good reason for not handing in work, you MUST inform your tutor before the deadline time, do not wait until after the deadline, the reason may not be accepted then.
