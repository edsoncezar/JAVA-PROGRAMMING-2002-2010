The constructor commences, on line 0036, by establishing a 7 by 7 GridLayout policy within which its 37 dayBoxes will be laid out. The first row is used for the dayNames labels and this establishes a size for the dayBoxes which will be large enough for them to contain their day numbers and borders.

The dayBoxes themselves are constructed and installed on lines 0043 to 0045 with each having the identity of the MonthPanel (this) passed as their listener attribute. The final action of the constructor is to register the identity of the MonthPanel's listener attribute.
